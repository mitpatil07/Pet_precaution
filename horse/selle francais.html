Selle Francais Personality

Selle Français horses are known for their athleticism, versatility, and gentle temperament. They excel in show jumping and dressage due to their natural agility, powerful build, and willingness to work. Their friendly nature makes them great partners for riders of all levels, and their intelligence allows them to learn quickly and perform well in various disciplines.

What to expect

The Selle Français is a versatile and athletic horse breed known for excelling in show jumping, dressage, eventing, and other competitive disciplines. They are typically intelligent, energetic, and willing to work, making them suitable for both amateur and professional riders. With proper training and care, Selle Français horses can perform well in a variety of equestrian activities and are often prized for their agility, strength, and competitive spirit.

History of the Selle Francais

The Selle Français, a renowned French sport horse breed, has a rich history rooted in the need for versatile and athletic horses. Originating in the mid-19th century, breeders sought to develop a horse suitable for both military and civilian purposes. They crossed local mares, often of Norman and Anglo-Norman descent, with Thoroughbred and Anglo-Arabian stallions to create a horse with speed, stamina, and jumping ability.
